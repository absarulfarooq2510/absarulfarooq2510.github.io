PM Philosophy

How I Think

The principles behind every product decision I make.

01
Outcome over Output
Success is measured by system behavior change and business impact — not feature launches. Shipping is the beginning, not the end.
02
Trust is a Product Requirement
In automated, AI-driven, or marketplace systems — if users don't trust the system, it fails regardless of accuracy. Trust is designed, not assumed.
03
Intelligence Before UI
Dashboards don't make decisions. The hard problem is knowing when and what to act on. I build the reasoning layer first.
04
Tradeoffs are First-Class
Every decision I document includes what was not built and why. Future teams deserve that context. Undocumented tradeoffs become technical debt.
